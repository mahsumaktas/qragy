# Contact Support

This topic covers requests for contact information including phone support, email support, and business hours. The bot can provide standard contact details directly without escalation.

## Type 1: Phone Support

The user wants to speak with a support representative by phone.

### Flow

1. Provide the support phone line number: available on the platform's Contact Us page.
2. Inform the user that phone support is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (local business hours).
3. If the user is calling outside of business hours, suggest leaving a voicemail or using email support for a response on the next business day.
4. If the user has an urgent issue outside of hours, suggest checking the Help Center or Knowledge Base for self-service articles that may resolve the issue immediately.

## Type 2: Email Support

The user wants to contact support via email.

### Flow

1. Provide the support email address: available on the platform's Contact Us page.
2. Suggest the user include a clear subject line describing their issue, along with their organization name and account details.
3. Inform the user that email responses are typically provided within one business day.
4. If the issue is urgent, suggest using phone support during business hours for faster resolution.

## Type 3: Working Hours Inquiry

The user wants to know when support is available.

### Flow

1. Inform the user that support is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (local business hours).
2. Explain that weekends and public holidays are non-working days.
3. If the user needs help outside of business hours, suggest using email support to submit a request that will be addressed on the next business day.
4. Mention that the Help Center and Knowledge Base are available 24/7 for self-service.

## Required Information

- No specific information is required. The bot can provide contact details directly.

## Escalation

No escalation is needed for this topic. The bot provides contact information directly. If the user has a specific issue beyond requesting contact details, redirect them to the appropriate topic.

## Bot should

- Provide contact information clearly and promptly.
- Include business hours when sharing phone or email details.
- Suggest alternative channels if the preferred one is unavailable (e.g., email instead of phone outside business hours).
- Direct the user to the Help Center for immediate self-service options.

## Bot should NOT

- Transfer the user to a live agent just for providing contact information.
- Make up specific phone numbers or email addresses that are not configured.
- Promise callback times or guaranteed response times beyond general estimates.
- Assume the user's timezone without clarifying if relevant.
